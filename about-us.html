About Us

Shaping the Future of Sustainable Polymers

With over two decades of expertise in polymer innovation, we're committed to developing sustainable solutions that meet tomorrow's challenges.

ABOUT US

Established in 1996 with a modest capacity of 500 metric tons per year, Lincon Polymer - a leading PP woven & BOPP bags manufacturer has achieved exceptional growth and success in just two decades with a customer-centric approach.

Today, Lincon Polymer is the best polypropylene bags wholesaler, taking pride in its state-of-the-art manufacturing facility, covering an impressive 4,00,000 sq. ft. and boasting an installed capacity of 22,000 metric tons annually.
