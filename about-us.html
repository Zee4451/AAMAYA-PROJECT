ABOUT US

Established in 1996 with a modest capacity of 500 metric tons per year, Aameya Polymer - a leading PP woven & BOPP bags manufacturer has achieved exceptional growth and success in just two decades with a customer-centric approach.

Today, Aameya Polymer is the best polypropylene bags wholesaler, taking pride in its state-of-the-art manufacturing facility, covering an impressive 4,00,000 sq. ft. and boasting an installed capacity of 22,000 metric tons annually.
