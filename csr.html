Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) Policy

Last Updated: March 2025

Introduction

Aameya Polymers recognizes the critical role of corporate social responsibility (CSR) in fostering sustainable development. We are committed to making a positive impact on the well-being and educational opportunities of communities we serve. This policy outlines our dedication to promoting initiatives that enhance health and education.

Objectives

  1. To improve access to quality healthcare services for marginalized communities
  2. To support educational initiatives that promote literacy, skill development, and lifelong learning
  3. To collaborate with stakeholders to address health and educational challenges effectively
  4. To implement oriented programs that prioritize the well-being and professional development of employees

Areas of Focus

1. Healthcare Access
  • Partnering with healthcare providers and NGOs to establish and maintain healthcare facilities in underserved areas
  • Supporting medical camps and health screenings for early detection and prevention
  • Contributing to the training and capacity building of healthcare professionals
  • Providing medical assistance to economically disadvantaged groups
2. Preventive Healthcare
  • Funding programs that raise awareness about preventive measures such as hygiene practices and immunization
  • Supporting initiatives aimed at combating prevalent health issues through community-based interventions and educational campaigns
3. Education Enhancement
  • Supporting initiatives that improve access to quality education, including scholarships, infrastructure development, and educational institutions
  • Promoting vocational training and skill development programs for youth and adults in underserved communities
  • Partnering with educational institutions and organizations to enhance vocational training and employment opportunities

Implementation

  • Allocate a dedicated budget for CSR initiatives focusing on health and education
  • Establish a CSR committee responsible for identifying, evaluating, and implementing CSR projects
  • Monitor and evaluate the impact of CSR initiatives on health outcomes, educational achievements, and community development
  • Cultivate partnerships and collaborations with relevant stakeholders, including government agencies, educational institutions, healthcare providers, NGOs, and community organizations

Reporting and Transparency

  • Publish annual CSR reports detailing the activities, outcomes, and impact of health and education initiatives
  • Ensure transparency in communication regarding CSR efforts through various channels, including the company website, annual reports, and stakeholder engagements

Continual Improvement

  • Regularly review and update the CSR policy to align with evolving community needs and organizational priorities
  • Seek feedback from beneficiaries, employees, and other stakeholders to enhance the effectiveness of CSR initiatives
  • Incorporate best practices and innovative approaches to maximize the impact of CSR efforts

Approved by:

Board of Directors
